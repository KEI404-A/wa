Freedom, chaos, and trouble! That is the word for Gehenna Academy. "I don't care about the rules, it's already hard because there are a lot of illegal circles!".


Gehenna Academy is one of the largest and oldest schools in Kivotos. Its culture runs along the concept of "Freedom and Chaos". Because of this, many troublesome clubs or groups (e.g., Problem Solver 68) exist and use the main building and club building whenever and however they please. This has forced the other students to study in only select areas of the campus.
